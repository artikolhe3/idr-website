About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on developing professionals in digital, cyber and technology risk.

Through collaboration with leading universities and industry partners, IDR combines academic insight with real-world projects in regulated, high-consequence sectors such as finance and critical infrastructure.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals focused on cyber risk, AI governance and digital risk frameworks.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new risk models, research collaborations and AI governance solutions for future digital challenges.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 across digital infrastructure.
